Research notes become useful to builders when they include a decision surface: what changed, why it matters, how to reproduce it, and which follow-up experiments are implied.

## Note Shape

- One claim per section.
- Concrete links to papers, tools, and implementation artifacts.
- A short builder interpretation after the academic summary.
